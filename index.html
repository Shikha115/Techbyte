About the Event

Jagan Institute of Management Studies has been highly successful in organizing IT Symposium on emerging technologies for the last 18 years. The focus is on proven technologies that have matured beyond the R&D stage, having wider applications and being commercialized especially to the advantage of the Indian Industry and society at large. This unique effort endeavours to act as a catalyst for technologies that will have a big impact on the overall economy of the country, increase productivity levels, speed of operation and also result in posting India high on the world technology levels. With this view, the symposium is designed to be a meeting point for policy makers, researchers, technology providers, end users, financers and knowledge seekers, associated with the technologies covered.

Symposium At Glance

Mentoring of Student/Incubating Technologies

We help students and emerging technologies grow by providing personalized mentorship and support, shaping them into valuable contributors to the tech world.

Industry-Academic Interface Units

These units serve as active hubs, linking education and industry, promoting collaboration, knowledge sharing, and practical application to ensure students are ready for the workforce.

Adjunct Faculty / Industry Sabbatical

Bringing real-world experience to education, adjunct faculty and industry sabbaticals enrich classrooms, offering practical insights and ensuring students learn from the latest industry developments.

Mechanism for Continuous Updates of Curriculum

Adapting to change, this system ensures that what students learn stays current. It updates the curriculum to match industry trends, providing students with the most relevant and recent knowledge.
